Who Am I?

I was born in Korea, but lived in Texas most of my life. I'm currently a senior at the University of Texas at Austin, and I'll be working in the cloud computing space upon graduating. I first learned how to code my sophomore year of college, and I've been passionate about programming and the tech industry ever since. On any given day, you can find me knitting, golfing, or reading.

My Free Time

I knit because I love the idea of creating things that I can wear and use everyday! I've never been very athletic, so I enjoy playing golf because it doesn't require a lot of strength or power. I love to read -- especially memoirs -- because I love hearing other people's stories.
